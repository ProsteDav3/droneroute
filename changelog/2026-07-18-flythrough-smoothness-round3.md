### Changed

- The 3D flythrough now also hides the citywide OpenStreetMap 3D-building extrusion, the flight path's ground-shadow line, its altitude poles, and POI-pointing lines while playing — none of them are something a real onboard camera would show, and the citywide buildings in particular can easily be the single heaviest thing on screen in a dense area (hundreds of extruded polygons unrelated to the mission). Buildings you've actually added to the mission stay visible.
