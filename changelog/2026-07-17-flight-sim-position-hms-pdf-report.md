### Fixed

- The "Simulace letu" button was invisible whenever a route warning (obstacle crossing, airspace conflict, home-distance...) was showing — it shared the exact same screen position as the warnings banner, which sits at a far higher stacking order. Moved the flight simulation control to the map's bottom-right corner, above the locate-me button.
- DJI Cloud health warnings showed a raw internal code (e.g. `fpv_tip_0x1610000F`) instead of a readable message, and the device panel only ever checked once whether a device was bound — a device that connected after the panel was already open never appeared without a full page reload. Warnings now show the platform's own English description, and the panel refreshes automatically every 20 seconds.
- The PDF flight report had three problems: Czech diacritics (čďěňřšťůž) rendered as garbled/missing characters because jsPDF's default font only covers Latin-1; the embedded map snapshot showed whatever the live map happened to be showing when you clicked the button, often zoomed out with the route barely visible; and the snapshot never showed waypoint markers at all, since those are DOM overlays the canvas capture never included. Reports now embed a proper Czech-safe font, automatically frame the snapshot to the whole route (restoring your original map view afterward), and draw each waypoint as a numbered marker on the map image.
