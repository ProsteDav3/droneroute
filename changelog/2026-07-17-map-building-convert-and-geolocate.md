### Added

- The map building popup now offers **Převést na budovu** alongside the existing **Převést na překážku**, so you can turn a building footprint from the map straight into a `Budova` object (for Facade scans, orbit-from-building, etc.) without redrawing it by hand.
- Added a "go to my location" control (bottom-right of the map). It centers the map on your current GPS position and keeps a small live dot there as you move, so you don't have to search an address just to find yourself on site.

### Fixed

- The undo/redo buttons overlapped the address search box in the top-left corner. They now sit below it.
