### Added

- Shift+click now accumulates adjacent 3D building fragments on the map into a selection — large complexes are often split into several separate footprints in OpenStreetMap. A new **Sloučit a převést na budovu** button merges the selection into one accurate `Budova` via a real polygon union (not a bounding box), so you don't have to convert each fragment one at a time and end up with several overlapping buildings for what is really one.
