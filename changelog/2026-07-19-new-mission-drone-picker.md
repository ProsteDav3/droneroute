### Added

- Clicking "Nová trasa" (new mission) now asks which drone you're flying before opening the editor, pre-selected to your account's default drone. A mission's drone/camera selection is saved with that mission alone — changing your account default afterward doesn't retroactively update missions you already made — so a pilot flying more than one drone model now confirms the right one per mission instead of discovering the wrong one later via a mismatched field-of-view calculation deep in the mission.
