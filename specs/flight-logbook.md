# Flight logbook

Keep a simple record of flights you've actually flown against a saved mission.

## What you can do

- Log a flight against any mission you've saved: date flown, duration in minutes, and a free-text note.
- View the list of logged flights for a mission, newest first.
- Delete a log entry you no longer want.

## Good to know

- This is basic record-keeping, not a full EU-compliant logbook — it doesn't capture every regulatory field (pilot license number, drone registration, incident details, and similar). Deeper regulatory-field coverage may be added later if there's demand for it.
- Log entries are private to your account. Only you can see or delete your own entries.
- You must save the mission before you can log a flight against it.
